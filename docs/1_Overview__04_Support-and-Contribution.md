# Support & Contribution

The Daily Mail Design System is a strategic product, not a shared asset library. It is actively maintained to ensure structural consistency, brand integrity, and long-term scalability across UK, US, and AU platforms.

To balance clarity of ownership with open collaboration, the following framework applies.

---

## 1. Getting Support

The Design System Owner provides primary support and direction for all system-related queries.

### Design Usage & Guidance

For any questions related to component usage, tokens, patterns, or structural decisions, please contact the Design System Owner in the first instance.

Where broader product context is needed, discussions may extend to members of the Design System Council to ensure squad-level considerations are appropriately reflected. This allows us to maintain consistency while remaining responsive to real product needs.

### Component or Token Issues

If a Figma component, variable, or token is not behaving as expected, please raise it directly with the Design System Owner or the Design System Council, including a **brief description** and **relevant context**. All issues are reviewed centrally to ensure fixes are applied consistently and do not introduce unintended downstream impact across all platforms.

When raising an issue, include:

- Screenshot
- Clear description of the issue
- Context of use (platform / surface)

The governance structure defines decision accountability, not hierarchy within the design team.
