# Overview

Foundation defines how design tokens are applied in real interface construction. It sets the structural baseline for layout, spacing, typography, elevation and motion.

> "The goal is not complexity, but clarity. Not restriction, but consistency."

By defining the default structure of the interface, Foundation helps designers move faster while maintaining coherence across the product.

## 1. What Foundation Is

Foundation is the structural logic layer of the Daily Mail Design System. It serves as the essential framework that translates abstract design values into a functional and cohesive user interface. While other layers may focus on individual elements, Foundation defines the environment in which those elements exist, providing the "connective tissue" that ensures structural integrity across every digital touchpoint.

Foundation is the bridge between raw values and functional components, ensuring every design decision is rooted in structural integrity rather than arbitrary choice.

---

## 2. The Role of Foundation

In a design system as vast as ours, the role of Foundation is to codify the invisible but rigorous rules of layout, spacing, and hierarchy. It acts as the definitive guide for how we manage information density, ensuring that our high-energy editorial content remains legible and authoritative. By establishing a shared spatial rhythm and layering logic, Foundation enables a unified brand experience across diverse platforms (Web, iOS, and Android) and global markets.

---

## 3. Why Foundation is Essential

The necessity of a robust Foundation lies in its ability to provide Operational Agility and Clarity at Scale. Without these fundamental rules, a large-scale product risks becoming a fragmented collection of components rather than a coherent editorial engine.

Foundation is required to:

- **Ensure Reading Endurance:** By applying scientific methods to spacing and typography, we protect the user's ability to consume high-density news without fatigue.
- **Standardise Decision-Making:** It removes arbitrary choices, allowing designers and developers to focus on storytelling and innovation rather than repetitive structural tasks.
- **Maintain Trusted Authority:** A predictable and disciplined interface reinforces Daily Mail's professional standards and builds long-term trust with our global audience.

---

## 4. How to Use this Foundation

### 4-A. A Living Standard, Not a Rigid Boundary

Foundation defines our "Gold Standard". However, we recognise that high-impact editorial storytelling sometimes requires flexibility. While adherence to these rules is expected for core product consistency, intentional deviation is permitted for bespoke features, provided it does not compromise the "Reading Endurance" or structural integrity of the interface.

### 4-B. Accessibility by Design

Inclusivity is not an additional layer; it is the baseline of our Foundation. Every spatial ratio, colour pairing, and typographic scale defined here has been pre-validated to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards. By following this Foundation, you are inherently designing for everyone.

### 4-C. Platform Specificity

While our Foundation provides a unified "Daily Mail" feel, it is engineered to respect the native behaviours of Web, iOS, and Android. This ensures that our global audience enjoys a seamless experience that feels familiar yet optimised for their specific device.
