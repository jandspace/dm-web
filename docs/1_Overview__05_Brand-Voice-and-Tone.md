# Brand Voice & Tone

Our visual language communicates the energy and authority of Daily Mail. These guidelines help us balance being "Popular" (engaging and accessible) with being "Serious" (trustworthy and professional).

---

## 1. Design Values

- **Bold but Balanced:** We use high-impact headlines and curated imagery, maintained within a disciplined grid to prevent visual clutter.
- **Urgent but Reliable:** Our UI reflects the speed of breaking news, yet every interaction is engineered to be stable and predictable.
- **Inclusive & Engaging:** We cater to a massive global audience without ever sacrificing legibility or reading endurance for the sake of aesthetics.

---

## 2. Visual Signature

- **Imagery:** Authentic, high-resolution photography that captures the raw emotion and truth of a story. We avoid generic visuals in favour of compelling storytelling.
- **Hierarchy:** Clear structural distinction between Lead Stories and Secondary Content to guide the reader's eye, especially within our high-density information environments.
- **Consistency as Trust:** We believe that a predictable and unified interface is the foundation of editorial authority.
